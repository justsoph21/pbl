This project is to give more information about myanmar traditional through an online website. In this project, we'll be talking about 6 topics. Fashion, which includes myanmar traditional dresses and how they use to dress in the old times. Weapon, which is about the weapons they used to protect themselves from all kind of things. Art, while includes about the amazing designs and paintings people did in the old times. City, which is about the places, landscapes, palaces, stations, museum and many more. Culture, which includes thanaka, which is a paste made from ground bark. Lotus Silk, The Leg Rowers of Inle, and many more. And lastly place. This category includes famous places like Pagodas, Temples, and many more like that.
